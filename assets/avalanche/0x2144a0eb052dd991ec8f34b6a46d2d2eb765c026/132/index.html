About Collection
An adorable collection of 1,000 Tiny Dragons ready for battle! Each attribute determines the stat value in strength and defense in our play-to-earn arena. The rarer your Tiny Dragon, the higher the stats value it will carry and the greater your chance of winning lucrative rewards!
